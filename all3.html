Date Sun 01 March 2015 By Mark Gondree, Zachary NJ Peterson

Alternate Reality Games (ARGs) are story-driven, trans-media art, designed to engage players to collaboratively uncover and interpret fragments of a story, distributed across multiple forms of media, using the “real world” as its platform. [d0x3d!]: a Network Security Board Game

Date Mon 01 September 2014 By Mark Gondree, Zachary NJ Peterson
